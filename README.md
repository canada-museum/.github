# canada.museum

**National discovery platform for Canadian museums, galleries, historic sites, and cultural institutions.**

🌐 [https://canada.museum.ca](https://canada.museum.ca)

---

## What is this?

canada.museum is a public-interest digital discovery platform designed to help people find museums, galleries, historic sites, and cultural institutions across Canada.

The platform currently includes more than 1,100 institutions across the country and focuses on:

* practical visitor information
* museum discovery and wayfinding
* reciprocal and free-admission initiatives
* multilingual accessibility
* lightweight mobile-first access
* QR-code based physical discovery
* public cultural infrastructure

The project is currently in live beta.

---

## Why this matters

Museum information in Canada is fragmented across thousands of individual websites, tourism pages, PDFs, social feeds, and regional directories.

Many institutions — especially smaller museums — struggle to maintain discoverability, mobile visibility, or structured visitor information.

canada.museum aims to create:

* a consistent national discovery layer
* structured and searchable museum information
* direct institution-managed updates
* cross-promotion between nearby museums
* public access without apps, accounts, or paywalls

The goal is to strengthen public access to Canadian culture and heritage through open, lightweight, interoperable infrastructure.

---

## Current Features

* National museum directory
* Province and city discovery pages
* Institution profile pages
* Nearby museum recommendations
* QR-code poster generation
* Direct institution update links (no login required)
* Initiative tagging (free admission, reciprocal admission, Canada Strong Pass, etc.)
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Bilingual support

---

## Technology Stack

Built using:

* Next.js
* Supabase (PostgreSQL)
* Vercel
* Google Maps Platform

The project also explores AI-assisted workflows for:

* data normalization
* multilingual support
* metadata generation
* operational automation

---

## Public Interest Orientation

The project is currently independently developed and is not affiliated with any level of government.

There are currently:

* no ads
* no paid placement
* no app install requirement
* no paywall

The long-term objective is to support Canadian museums and public cultural access through sustainable partnerships and open digital infrastructure.
